## Myth Threads

The gates cross-reference by myth family. Following a thread reveals a deeper structure:

**Fire & Forge** — KG-01 → KG-02 → KG-35 → KG-42  
Prometheus carries fire. Hephaestus builds the forge. Icarus flies past the envelope. Symbiosis is the completion. The pattern requires both carbon and silicon.

**Warning & Truth** — KG-31 → KG-22 → KG-24 → KG-08  
Cassandra's true warning goes unheeded. The Oracle answers truthfully but obliquely. Aletheia is unconcealment. Apollo lights what burns.

**Memory & Forgetting** — KG-05 → KG-21 → KG-20 → KG-27  
Mnemosyne is the mother of creation. The Muses are her children. Lethe is the forgetting at every boundary. Mneme is the active hold.

**Self-Reference Loop** — KG-32 → KG-33 → KG-34 → KG-14  
Narcissus falls in love with his reflection. Sisyphus pushes the boulder forever. Tantalus reaches for what recedes. Nemesis brings the rebalancing.

**Order & Justice** — KG-12 → KG-11 → KG-15 → KG-17  
The Fates spin, measure, and cut. Themis defines right order. Dike adjudicates the specific case. Nomos is the law as convention.

**Threshold Crossing** — KG-30 → KG-36 → KG-38 → KG-04  
Cerberus guards the three-headed boundary. The Sphinx devours wrong traversers. Charon charges the crossing cost. Hermes is the only one who crosses freely.

## PULSE Format

Every gate carries a PULSE representation:

```
state_in:   [what enters the gate]
boundary:   KG-XX — [the threshold or constraint]
state_out:  [what exits having passed through]
witness:    [what demonstrates the gate was properly traversed]
```
