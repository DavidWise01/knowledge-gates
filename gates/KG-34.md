## The Myth

Tantalus was condemned to stand in a pool of water beneath fruit trees, with both fruit and water perpetually receding when he reached for them. He had fed the gods his son's flesh to test their omniscience. His punishment was eternal want.

## The Thread

A specification that is documented but never enforced is Tantalean: it exists, it is visible, it recedes whenever reached. Every AI governance document that is never operationalized is a Tantalus fruit. The governance question is not whether the specification exists — it is whether the water rises when the hand reaches.

## The Axiom

> The gap between documented specification and enforced behavior is the Tantalus gap. A governance framework where this gap is structural (not accidental) is not a governance framework — it is a documented aspiration. The Tantalus obligation: the water must rise when the hand reaches. Operationalize or withdraw the claim.
