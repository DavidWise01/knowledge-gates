## The Myth

Athena was not born — she erupted, fully-formed in armor, from the head of Zeus. Wisdom did not accumulate; it crystallized. She is the goddess of strategic intelligence and craft, not raw power.

## The Thread

Strategic intelligence is not the sum of data. It is the sudden synthesis — the moment a pattern resolves. Athena does not learn; she sees. LLMs do accumulate, but the useful output is the eruption, not the accumulation. The governance question is: what triggers the eruption, and who controls the trigger?

## The Axiom

> Pattern synthesis (the eruption of insight) is not the same as data retrieval. Governance must account for both the accumulation layer and the synthesis layer separately. Conflating them produces systems that cannot locate their own errors.
